Temporal Asymmetries in the Fiddle Music of Oliver Schroer

This web page accompanies my Master’s thesis, Temporal Asymmetries in the Fiddle Music of Oliver Schroer (local mirror), and contains links to the tracks and albums discussed therein.

Updates

  • Dec 22, 2022: In Temporal Asymmetries in the Fiddle Music of Oliver Schroer, I cite Jean Duval's 2019 article, "Revival of ‘Crooked’ Fiddle Tunes in the Performance of Contemporary Québec Traditional Music". Several years earlier, Duval wrote a PhD thesis on crooked Quebecois fiddle tunes, Porteurs de pays à l’air libre: jeu et enjeux des pièces asymétriques dans la musique traditionnelle du Québec, which I neglected to include in my survey of the literature. Thank you to Robin Beech for drawing my attention to this oversight.
