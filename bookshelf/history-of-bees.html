The History of Bees – Maja Lunde

Thoughts: The History of Bees traces the intertwined stories of a 19th-century biologist, a modern-day beekeeper, and a couple living in a post-apocalyptic future. This one was suggested by Simon Clark, and it didn't disappoint.
