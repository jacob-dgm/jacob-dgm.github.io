Bloodletting & Miraculous Cures – Vincent Lam

Comments: Found this collection of linked short stories—about a group of medical students, which read somewhat like successive chapters, though they don’t come together to form a unifying narrative—in a book box in Kitchener. The stories are compelling, if a bit morbid.

Lam, Vincent. 2005. Bloodletting & Miraculous Cures. Anchor Canada.
