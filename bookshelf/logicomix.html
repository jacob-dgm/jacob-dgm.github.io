Logicomix: An Epic Search for Truth – Apostolos Doxiadis and Christos H. Papadimitriou

Thoughts: Logicomix is a graphic novel dramatizing the struggle of Bertrand Russel, Alfred North Whitehead and others to prove logic and mathematics to be complete and consistent. An apt suggestion by Neil Guertin upon hearing that I had enjoyed reading Gödel, Escher, Bach, I finished it in about a day.

Doxiadis, Apostolos and Christos H. Papadimitriou, illus. Alecos Papadatos and Annie Di Donna. 2009. Logicomix: An Epic Search for Truth. Bloomsbury.
