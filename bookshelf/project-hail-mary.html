Project Hail Mary – Andy Weir

Thoughts: A recommendation by my friend Nicole, who was spot on. Exhilirating. I appreciated how scientifically informed the book was, similar to Hank Green’s An Absolutely Remarkable Thing and A Beautifully Foolish Endeavor.

(The notes below are not a summary of the book, but rather raw notes - whatever I thought, at the time, might be worth remembering. I read this as an e-book, so page numbers are as they appeared in the app I used, Libby.)

Weir, Andy. 2021. Project Hail Mary. Ballantine Books.

  • 356: “Science rule number 1: If something is changing unexpectedly, document it.”
